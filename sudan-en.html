Sudan

Land of ancient pyramids and rich cultural heritage

About Sudan

Sudan is the largest country in Africa and the Arab world, located in northeastern Africa. It is characterized by its rich history, diverse culture, and stunning natural landscapes.

The country is home to numerous archaeological sites, including the ancient pyramids of Meroë, and features diverse geographical regions from the Nile Valley to the Red Sea coast.
