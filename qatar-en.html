Qatar

A modern Arab country that combines tradition with innovation

About Qatar

Qatar is a modern Arab country located on the northeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula. It is distinguished by its rapid development and its ability to balance between preserving its cultural heritage and embracing modernity.

Qatar has become a global hub for business, tourism, and sports, hosting major international events such as the FIFA World Cup 2022. The country is known for its futuristic architecture, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality.
