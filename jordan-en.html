Jordan

Home to ancient civilizations and stunning natural landscapes

About Jordan

Jordan is an Arab country located in the heart of the Middle East, distinguished by its rich history and diverse geography. It is home to numerous archaeological sites and natural wonders.

The country features diverse landscapes including the Dead Sea, Wadi Rum desert, and the ancient city of Petra. Jordan is known for its warm hospitality and has become a major tourist destination in the region.
